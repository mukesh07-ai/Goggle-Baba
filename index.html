About Us

A.B. MEDIA ACADEMY was established in 2020 in response to the fervent requests from members of the Asia Pacific Broadcasting Union (ABU).
As a non-profit, non-governmental,
and professional association,
the ABU has been championing the development of broadcasting in the region since its inception in 1964.





Our Mission


To realize this vision, the ABU Media Academy collaborates closely with member organizations. Our mission encompasses three key pillars:



  • 1. Enhanced In-House Training:

    • We work hand-in-hand with ABU members to strengthen their internal training programs. By tailoring content to their specific needs, we empower their teams to excel.

  • 2. Access to International Experts

    • Through the ABU Media Academy, members gain access to external training led by international experts. From cutting-edge technologies to industry best practices, we ensure that learning transcends borders.

  • 3. Tertiary Education Opportunities

    • We facilitate further study for staff and management within recognized tertiary institutions. Lifelong learning is at the core of our ethos, and we extend this opportunity to non-members as well.

Our Vision

At the heart of our vision lies the belief that every ABU member can empower their staff to become multiskilled, adaptable, and well-versed in the latest trends and technologies within our dynamic industries. We envision a media landscape where professionals thrive, equipped with the knowledge and expertise to shape the future of broadcasting.

Content Writing

The Content Writing course equips students with skills to produce compelling and effective written content. It covers various writing styles, techniques for different platforms and audiences, SEO optimization, and storytelling principles.

Media Production:

This course at A.B. Media Academy covers the creation and production of media content. Students will learn about the different stages of media production, including pre-production, production, and post-production processes to create high-quality audiovisual content.

Anchoring

Anchoring refers to being a television news anchor. This course would teach students about presenting news stories on TV effectively, including aspects like voice modulation, screen presence, and script reading.

Reporting

Reporting involves gathering information and creating news reports. The course may include training on investigative skills, interviewing techniques, and ethical considerations in journalism.

Video Editing

In this course, students learn about editing videos to create polished visual content. Topics include using editing software tools, cutting & arranging footage sequences; adding effects & transitions; color correction; audio editing; etc.

Camera (Photography/Videography/
Cinematography)

These courses focus on camera operation for still photography or filming videos/movies respectively. Topics could range from camera settings adjustments to advanced cinematographic techniques for storytelling through visuals.

Video Jockey (VJ)

Aspiring VJs will be trained in presenting music videos interviews live shows etc., focusing on presentation style audience engagement scriptwriting video aesthetics camera presence among other key areas.

Radio Jockey (RJ)

This course prepares students for a career as an RJ covering voice modulation scripting hosting radio shows understanding radio equipment audience engagement ethics in broadcasting etc.

Modeling

Modeling at A.B Media Academy provides insights into the world of fashion modeling including catwalk techniques photo shoot preparation portfolio development self-presentation grooming standards industry insights etc.
